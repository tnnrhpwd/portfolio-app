Tips for accurate calibration:
  • Sit ~50–70 cm from the screen
  • Face should be well-lit from the front (avoid backlight)
  • If you wear glasses, keep them on; recalibrate if you remove them
  • Keep your head level and centered — don't tilt
  • Stare at the exact center of each dot
  • The dot will turn green when it captures a stable reading
