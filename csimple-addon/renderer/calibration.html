How To Start Training
1. Choose webcam and monitor.
2. Pick a training mode.
3. Click Begin Calibration and follow every dot.
say: calibrate eye tracking say: track my eyes say: stop eye tracking
Tips for accurate calibration:
  • Sit ~50–70 cm from the screen
  • Face should be well-lit from the front (avoid backlight)
  • If you wear glasses, keep them on; recalibrate if you remove them
  • Vary your head pose slightly while looking at each dot — small left/right turns and up/down nods (~5° either way). This teaches the system to compensate when your head moves later, dramatically improving accuracy outside the calibration pose.
  • Stare at the exact center of each dot
  • The dot will turn green when it captures a stable reading
Advanced camera options
Auto: 640×480 processing, driver-default capture. Works for most webcams.
IR: Applies CLAHE contrast boost, relaxed detection thresholds, 1080p capture with sub-pixel iris refinement. Use for Windows Hello / UVC IR cameras.
Hi-res / 4K: Captures at 4K, processes at 1280×720 with sub-pixel iris refinement for maximum precision.
